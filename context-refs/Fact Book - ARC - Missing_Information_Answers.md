## 8. Problem Statements

### Critical Problems ARC Addresses:

1. **Critical Materials as National Vulnerability**
   - U.S. remains exposed to foreign concentration in rare earths, magnets, and advanced industrial inputs
   - Domestic supply chains for exotic materials are brittle

2. **Manufacturing Qualification is Slow and Expensive**
   - Validating new alloys/ceramics and processes can take years
   - Limits iteration and scaling
   - Traditional: 10-12 years from lab research to deployable production

3. **AI Alone Doesn't Solve It**
   - Without high-quality data and a path to manufacturing execution, compute-driven insights don't translate into production outcomes
   - Industrial manufacturing has yet to fully exploit modern AI and HPC

4. **AI Compute Concentration**
   - Current AI development heavily concentrated in a few industry labs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind)
   - "Tight control over access to specialized infrastructure... will likely result in a concentrated generative AI market controlled by a few key players"
