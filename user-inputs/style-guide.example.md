# Investor Deck Style Guide - ARC Example

This is an example style guide for Autonomous Resource Corporation (ARC).

## Voice & Tone

### Overall Tone

Tone: Authoritative

### Language Style

- Sentence length: Medium (15-25 words average)
- Jargon level: Industry-standard for deep tech / infrastructure investors
- Active voice: Yes
- Person: "ARC" (third person), "you/your" for investor benefits

## Terminology

### Preferred Terms

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---------------|--------|
| data center | AI factory |
| GPU cluster | AI compute infrastructure |
| 3D printing | additive manufacturing |
| superalloy | advanced materials |
| machine learning | AI (when general) |

### Company-Specific Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| ARCNet | ARC's network of AI factory sites |
| ADAM | Autonomous Discovery of Advanced Materials platform |
| AI-to-atom | End-to-end from AI compute to physical manufacturing |
| AI factory | Purpose-built 100MW+ AI compute facility |

### Forbidden Terms

- "Revolutionary"
- "Disruptive"
- "Game-changing"
- "Best-in-class"
- "World-class"
- "Synergy"

## Visual Preferences

### Color Usage

- Primary (1E3A5F - Navy): Headlines, ARC branding, key metrics
- Secondary (4A90D9 - Blue): Subheadings, charts, supporting elements
- Accent (F5A623 - Gold): Important numbers, CTAs, highlights
- Dark (1F2937): Body text
- Light (F8FAFC): Backgrounds

### Data Visualization Style

- Prefer: Clean bar charts, simple timelines, nested circles for TAM/SAM/SOM
- Chart types: Horizontal bars for comparisons, timeline for milestones
- Avoid: Pie charts (except use-of-funds), complex diagrams

### Image Generation Guidelines

- Style: Professional, technical, slightly futuristic
- Color palette: Navy/blue/gold brand colors
- Imagery themes: Data centers, manufacturing, AI visualization
- Text in images: Minimal, large sans-serif

## Content Guidelines

### Slide Text Limits

- Title slides: 10 words max
- Headlines: 6 words max
- Content slides: 3 bullets max
- Bullet points: 12 words max
- Stat callouts: Number + 4-word label

### Citation Style

- In-deck: [Source: Document Name] in gray, small text
- Data freshness: 2024-2026 data only

### Numbers & Metrics

- Format: $1.2B, 10MW, 200+ experiments/week
- Percentages: Whole numbers (98%, not 98.3%)
- Currency: USD

## Competitor References

- Name in competition slide only
- Position ARC in upper-right quadrant (open + industrial execution)
- Competitors: OpenAI, AWS, Google Cloud, Anthropic

## Slide-Specific Guidelines

### Title Slide

- "Autonomous Resource Corporation" prominent
- Tagline: "AI-to-atom infrastructure for national manufacturing advantage"
- No funding details

### Problem Slide

- 3 pain points: qualification speed, compute access, power demand
- Key stat: "12 months, $500K-$800K" for traditional qualification

### Solution Slide

- Value prop: "Open AI infrastructure + autonomous manufacturing"
- Benefits: 3-4x timeline compression, 20x experiment scale, 10x cost reduction

### Market Size Slide

- Note: Market sizing TBD - use demand signals instead
- Key signal: "+150-250 TWh by 2030" for AI data centers

### Team Slide

- Bryan Wisk (CEO), Paul Adams (President), Dr. Leo Christodoulou (CTO)
- One-line backgrounds emphasizing relevant experience
